          About Us

          Welcome to Jottie

          Jottie is a comprehensive student productivity platform designed to help students organize their academic life, manage tasks, and boost productivity. Easily jot down your events and tasks!

          Mission

          Jottie is committed to empower students with intuitive tools that simplify the academic journey —an all-in-one solution that puts organization at your fingertips.

          Our Features

          Calendar

          Never miss a deadline with the intuitive calendar system. Schedule assignments, exams, and study sessions with color-coded events.

          To-Do List

          Break down complex projects into manageable tasks. The priority-based task management system helps you focus on what matters most.

          School Binder

          Keep your notes organized by subject. The digital binder replicates the convenience of physical notes with the power of digital search and organization.

          Dashboard

          Get a bird's-eye view of your academic progress and upcoming priorities with the customizable dashboard.

          Background

          Jottie was created by a cpe student who understands firsthand the challenges of staying organized throughout academic life. Combining experience in education, design, and technology, I built Jottie as the tool I wished I had during my studies. Jottie is inspired by Notion and Folderly.
